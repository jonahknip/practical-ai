## INSTRUCTIONS

This inventory helps identify and prioritize AI automation opportunities based on:
1. **Time wasted** - How many hours per week
2. **Employee pain** - How frustrating (1-10 scale)
3. **Error impact** - Quality/cost of mistakes
4. **AI potential** - How automatable (Low/Med/High)
5. **Quick win** - Can we show results in 30 days?

---

## DISCOVERY QUESTIONS

Use these during employee shadowing/interviews:

### General Questions
- What tasks take up most of your time?
- What work feels repetitive or tedious?
- What would you do with an extra 10 hours per week?
- What processes frequently have errors or quality issues?
- What tasks do you dread doing?
- Where do you copy-paste the same information repeatedly?
- What reports or documents do you create manually that follow a template?
- What information do you have to look up in multiple systems?

### Specific Process Questions
- Walk me through this task step-by-step
- How long does this typically take?
- How often do you do this?
- What goes wrong with this process?
- How much time do you spend fixing errors?
- If you could wave a magic wand, what would change?
