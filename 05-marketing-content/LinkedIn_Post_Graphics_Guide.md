# LinkedIn Post Graphics Guide
**Goal:** Create clean, high-impact LinkedIn visuals in ~10 minutes each.
**Tool:** Canva (recommended)

---

## Global Design Specs

**Dimensions:**
- Square: **1080 x 1080** (primary)
- Horizontal: **1200 x 628** (optional for ads)

**Color Palette:**
- Navy: **#1B3A5C** (headlines, strong emphasis)
- Blue: **#3B82F6** (accent, numbers, CTA)
- Gray: **#4A5568** (body text)
- Light Gray: **#E2E8F0** (background, lines)
- White: **#FFFFFF** (background)

**Fonts (Canva):**
- Primary: **Inter** (clean, professional)
- Alternate: **Poppins** (modern, bold)

**Text Hierarchy:**
- **Headline:** 60–80 pt, Navy, bold
- **Stat Number:** 90–110 pt, Blue, bold
- **Body:** 28–36 pt, Gray
- **Footer/Attribution:** 22–26 pt, Gray

**Canva Links:**
- Canva: https://www.canva.com
- Search: "LinkedIn post 1080x1080"
